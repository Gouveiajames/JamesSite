Race Car Driver

My Mom won tickets to see a Trans Am race at Sears Point. My Dad took me to the races and I remember seeing the rainex car of Scott Sharp win the race with the Hot Wheels car of Jack Baldwin coming in second. That was it, I was hooked at 11 years old! All I have ever wanted to do after that is race cars. I am between race cars at the moment but I hope to have another car soon!
